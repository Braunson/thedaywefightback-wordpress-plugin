== Description ==

thedaywefightback.js is a javascript snippet that automatically activates at midnight of February 11th and adds a banner to your site for 24 hours in support of thedaywefightback.org. The goal is to drive as many phone calls and emails on the 11th. The calls are connected automatically by our call server for free thanks to Twilio.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `thedaywefightback-banner` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Your all set!
